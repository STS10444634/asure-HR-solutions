THE FOUNDER

NALEDI MOTLHABANE

Profile

Naledi Motlhabane is the Founder, Director, and CEO of ASURE HR SOLUTIONS, a 100% Black female-owned HR consulting firm. She is a seasoned Human Resources professional with over 15 years of experience in HR and labour relations.

Qualifications & Industry Exposure

She holds an Honours degree in Industrial & Organisational Psychology and has extensive experience across industries including mining, higher education, petrochemical, telecommunications, call centre operations, and retail.

Purpose & Vision

Naledi founded ASURE HR SOLUTIONS to provide professional HR support to small and medium-sized businesses without in-house HR capacity, helping them navigate labour legislation and build effective, compliant workplaces.

Leadership & Approach

With a hands-on, practical approach, Naledi delivers HR solutions that are fully compliant with South African labour laws and tailored to each client's operational needs, driven by integrity, professionalism, and a strong client-focused mindset.

Under Naledi's leadership, ASURE HR Solutions offers a grounded, hands-on approach, partnering with clients to deliver legally sound HR solutions tailored to the realities of South African workplaces.
